Who I Am

About Me

I'm a recent graduate with a Master of Science in Information Systems and an MBA, with a strong interest in how data can help organizations understand what’s really happening inside their systems and operations. My work sits at the intersection of analytics, technology, and business decision-making.

Through my academic projects and professional experience, I’ve worked with tools like SQL, Python, Tableau, and Power BI to analyze performance, build dashboards, and turn complex datasets into insights that teams can actually use. I enjoy bridging the technical and business sides of problems — translating data into something clear, meaningful, and actionable.

Outside of work, I'm curious about AI adoption trends, data storytelling, and how organizations build cultures that actually use the data they collect. I'm also a certified yoga practitioner, and I value the balance it brings — helping me stay focused, thoughtful, and grounded in both work and life.
